Deeds Checklist

Here are thirty things you could aim to do this Ramadan.

Feed a fasting person
Donate to a charity/donate unused items to the local masjid
Help your family with chores around the house
Pay your Zakat al-Fitr!
Spend time with your family/younger siblings
Go one day without complaining
Pray tahajjud and make du'a for someone going through hardship
Show your kindness to everyone, including animals
Forgive others and, most importantly, yourself
Engage in dhikr
Make a du'a list
Spend your day solely in remembrance of Allah (SWT)
Send an uplifting message to someone in need
Commit to giving up a bad habit and ask Allah (SWT) for forgiveness
Call a family member/friend who you've not spoken to in a while
Learn one name of Allah (SWT) and invoke Him with it
Share your knowledge!
Memorise/learn a new surah
Stand in prayer on Laylatul Qadr
Make an Eid card/share a gift with neighbours, family and friends
