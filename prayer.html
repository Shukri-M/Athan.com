Prayers

Prayer (Salah) in Islam is a fundamental act of worship, providing a direct connection between the worshipper and Allah. It instills discipline, spiritual growth, and a sense of community among Muslims. By removing ego and disciplining oneself to pray on time, prayer reinforces faith, gratitude, and reliance on Allah, shaping moral and ethical behavior in daily life.

Fajr

The pre-dawn prayer. It is the first of the five daily prayers.

Dhuhr

The midday prayer. It is the second of the five daily prayers.

Asr

The afternoon prayer. It is the third of the five daily prayers.

Maghrib

The sunset prayer. It is the fourth of the five daily prayers.

Isha

The night prayer. It is the fifth and last of the five daily prayers.
