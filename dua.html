Dua in Islam

Dua (supplication) is a significant act of worship in Islam, representing a personal conversation with Allah. It encompasses requests, praises, and expressions of gratitude, reflecting a believer's dependence on and trust in Allah.

Importance of Dua

Dua is a powerful way for Muslims to communicate with Allah, seek guidance, and ask for help. It signifies a believer's submission and reliance on Allah's wisdom and mercy.
